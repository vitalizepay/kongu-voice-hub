District at a glance

🏆 Top Turmeric Producer Erode produces 43% of Tamil Nadu's turmeric, recognized with GI tag. Also major hub for plantain, coconut & white silk.
🧵 Textile powerhouse Over 23,000 small industries power handlooms & powerlooms — knitwear and home textiles exported worldwide.
🌾 Cauvery lifeline Situated on western bank of river Kaveri, the district is known for Bhavani Sagar dam & fertile agriculture belt.
🌡️ Today’s weather extreme IMD reports 40.0°C peak in Erode city; dry and hot interior conditions expected for next 48 hours.
