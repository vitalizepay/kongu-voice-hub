About Coimbatore

Often referred to as the "Manchester of South India", Coimbatore is a major industrial, commercial, and educational hub in Tamil Nadu. Known for its textile mills, engineering firms, and auto component manufacturing, the district also boasts picturesque locations like Isha Yoga Center, Marudamalai Temple, and Kovai Kutralam Falls.

With a rapidly growing IT corridor, Coimbatore is emerging as a preferred destination for startups and global enterprises. The district is also the gateway to the scenic hill stations of Ooty, Mettupalayam, and Valparai.

Key Highlights

  • Agriculture: Coconut, turmeric, & vegetables – major producer.
  • Tech Hub: STPI Coimbatore, TIDEL Park, KCT Tech Park.
  • Connectivity: Coimbatore Int'l Airport (CJB) with direct flights to Middle East, Singapore.
  • Cultural Gems: VOC Park, Gedee Car Museum, Brookefields Mall.
  • Education: PSG, Amrita, Bharathiar University, and more.
